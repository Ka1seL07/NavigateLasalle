DLSU-D joins Study Abroad Fair in Taiwan

MARCH 14, 2024 NEWS OFFICE OF THE VICE PRESIDENT FOR INTERNATIONALIZATION AND EXTERNAL AFFAIRS

DLSU-D’s Linkages and Scholarship Office recently participated in the Partner Day and Study Abroad Fair organized by Providence University in Taiwan.

The activity aims to enhance the presence of foreign students at DLSU-D through its regular program offerings and exchange programs. Representing the university were Dr. Sonia Gementiza, Dr. George Francisco, and Mr. Romano Angelico Ebron.

During the event, DLSU-D was the only Philippine university that presented its global initiatives to various foreign institutions. The two-day global event highlighted the university’s internationalization programs, including academic mobility, foreign admissions, and social events among university officials, diplomatic corps, and other stakeholders.
